About identex

The app for bars

identex is a tool that was created for businesses to feel more confident in dealing with age-restricted products. Our mission is to ensure the highest-possible accuracy in helping to determine valid ID's, as well as giving our customers the tools and analytics they need to grow into stronger businesses.

Our company values integrity, responsibility, privacy, and the safety of our community. Our customers are businesses who are interested in being safe, smart, and sure about who they are selling age-restricted products to. (Restaurants, bars, tobacco vendors, etc.), and they know the value of being able to access metrics from their customer base at a moment's notice.

Dashboard

identex provides your business with a dashboard that gives you the control to manage your subscription, see your customer data, and make conclusions about how your business is doing. We also provide a live chat line on the desktop dashboard to offer any assistance that you may need.

User Friendly

We designed identex with you in mind. With simple navigation and intuitive design, checking an ID has never been easier.

Numbers Made Easy

With Identex it is easy to understand the numbers behind your business, which empowers you, the business owner, to make smart decisions on how to expand.

Cloud Functionality
